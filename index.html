How we choose our picks

We don’t run lab tests or time trials. Instead, we spend dozens of hours combing through owner reviews on major retail sites, forum discussions, and published roundups from editorial outlets that do hands-on testing. We look for patterns: which models hold up after six months? Which ones have complaints about rust, wobbly dividers, or compartments that don’t fit standard office supplies? Our picks are the ones that repeatedly show up in “bought this three years ago and it’s still perfect” comments and are recommended by experts who don’t have a financial stake in the brand. We also check for simple deal-breakers – poor weld quality, sharp edges, and dividers that snap off – so you can buy with confidence without having to wade through a thousand reviews yourself.
